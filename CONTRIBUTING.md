# Contributing to Prowtein

Thanks for your interest in Prowtein.

**Contributions are not open at this time.** The project is in early development and the codebase is changing rapidly. External PRs won't be reviewed.

You are free to:
- Fork the repository and build on it under the MIT license
- Open issues to report bugs or suggest ideas
- Star the repo and follow along

Once the project stabilizes, this policy will be revisited.
